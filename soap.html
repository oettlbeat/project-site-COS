making SOAP

There is nothing better than the smell of fresh handmade soap made out of natural ingredients and essential oils. Making soap is not only a meditative and relaxing task, it also gives you the chance to chose your ingredients to your likings. Plus, handmade soap is a low waste alternative to store bought and makes a great gift for nearly every occasion.

Materials - What you need

The most important ingredients in a soap are oil and lye. You can chose from a variety of different oils as a base depending on your preferences. To top it up and make your soap unique, you can use essential oils, flower petals and spices.

Here are some examples of ingredients:

  • Coconut oil
  • Cocoa butter
  • Castor oil
  • Lye (can be found in a pharmacy)
  • Lavender essential oil

List of tools

Although making soap is a straightforward process that only uses a few ingredients, there are some basic tools which are necessary to make soap. Also don't forget to take safety precautions and always use gloves and goggles.

  • Safety gloves
  • Safety goggles
  • Soap pot
  • Kitchen scale
  • Glass pitcher
  • Mason jar with lid
  • Plastic pitcher with lid
  • Thermometer
  • Measuring cups
  • Spoons
  • Spatula
  • Stick blender
  • Molds
